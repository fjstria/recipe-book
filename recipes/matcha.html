Matcha Blondies

Description

Matcha blondies are a delightful twist on the classic brownie, swapping out the traditional cocoa powder for vibrant matcha powder. These indulgent treats feature a rich base that perfectly complements the earthy, slightly bitter notes of matcha. The result is a unique, green-hued dessert with a chewy texture and a complex flavor profile that balances sweetness with the distinctive taste of matcha. Perfect for tea lovers and anyone looking to explore new dessert flavors, matcha blondies offer a refreshing and sophisticated alternative to standard brownies, making them a standout addition to any dessert spread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp matcha green tea powder
  • 2 sticks (1 cup) melted salted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine flour, white sugar, and matcha powder.
  2. Add butter, beaten eggs, vanilla, and milk. Mix until a batter forms. If the batter is dry, add milk by the tablespoon until the mixture reaches the desired consistency.
  3. Add white chocolate chips, or other mix-ins, as desired.
  4. Bake at 350ÂºF for 35 minutes.
